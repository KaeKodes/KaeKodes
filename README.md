UX and AI Developer

From graduating with my bachelor's in psychology to pursuing my master's in computer science, I know what it means to keep people in mind with everything you do while also changing the way technology is viewed in the world of A.I. I've been designing accessible and creative user experiences for ALL since 2020. In 2022, I found a brand-new love for coding new experiences for others. I began to dive into the depths of software engineering and building AI models that provides security for those that use it.
